The Challenge

The Oscars100 Hut2Hut is a 100 km loop race held during the 2026 Hut 2 Hut Weekend. The course has ~5,700 m of elevation gain, passing 10 climbs and 8 historic huts, looping from and returning to Mt Buller, Victoria.

  • Race: Oscars100 Hut2Hut
  • Date: 20–22 February 2026
  • Distance: 100 km
  • Elevation Gain: ~5,700 m
  • Climbs: 10
  • Huts passed: 8
  • Location: Mt Buller, VIC
